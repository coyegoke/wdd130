White Water Rafting

Salmon River Experience first applied for our outfitters’ license in 1978 with the goal of providing the highest quality, affordable whitewater adventures available, which are both fun and adventurous while at the same time sensitive to the cultural and environmental needs of the river.

History

The sport of whitewater rafting was in its infant stages in 1962. That spring, George Armstrong placed a small raft into the Stanislaus River near Yosemite National Park and ventured into the unknown. A lot has been learned since those early days of rafting, but the same joy and excitement initially experienced on the river has only grown.

More than 60 years of rafting on California rivers is enough time to experience anything that can happen on a whitewater rafting trip. Organizing over 40,000 trips is the best way to learn how to put on amazing rafting adventures! Taking 450,000 guests down rivers is a great way to learn how to care for folks as you lead them through river canyons.
